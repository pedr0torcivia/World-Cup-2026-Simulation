# Variable Catalog

This catalog defines the important variables used by the simulator. Scales are deliberately moderate: the model should produce probabilities, uncertainty and sensitivity, not deterministic claims.

| Variable | Definition | Scale | Expected data source | Default if missing | Impact formula | Module | Outputs | Minimum tests |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `team_strength` | Latent pre-match team quality before context. | 0-1 index | FIFA ranking points, Elo-like ratings, squad value, player ratings, form, experience, coach/tactical data | Derived from `elo_rating`, `fifa_ranking`, `squad_depth`, `recent_form`, `experience` | Weighted sum from `team_strength_weights` | `src/simulation/expected_goals.py` | data quality report, sensitivity inputs | `tests/test_expected_goals.py` |
| `attack_strength` | Ability to generate expected goals. | Multiplicative, centered on 1.0 | xG for, goals, shots, big chances, box entries, set-piece xG | Derived from `attack_rating` and `team_strength` | Weighted attack index plus shrunk finishing | `src/simulation/expected_goals.py` | xG per match, score matrix | `tests/test_expected_goals.py` |
| `defense_strength` | Ability to suppress opponent expected goals. | Multiplicative, centered on 1.0 | xG against, shots allowed, pressing success, aerial defense | Derived from `defense_rating` | `defense_weakness = 1 / defense_strength` | `src/simulation/expected_goals.py` | xG against | `tests/test_expected_goals.py` |
| `goalkeeper_strength` | Goal prevention and penalty impact. | 0.75-1.25 | save %, PSxG-GA, penalty saves, errors | Derived from `goalkeeper_rating` | `1 - goalkeeper_weight * (gk_strength - 1)` | `src/simulation/expected_goals.py`, `penalties.py` | xG adjustment, shootout probability | `tests/test_expected_goals.py`, `tests/test_penalties.py` |
| `player_impact_score` | Player value adjusted for role, availability and replaceability. | Rating-like score | Player data provider, scouting | Team-level fallback if no players | `overall * importance * starter_probability * position_weight * availability` | `src/simulation/player_impact.py` | key player loss risk | `tests/test_data_quality.py` |
| `squad_depth_index` | Ability to absorb rotation, injuries and suspensions. | 0-1 | Player ratings 1-26, market data | Derived from `squad_depth` | `1 - normalized_replacement_gap` | `src/simulation/expected_goals.py`, injuries/fatigue | injury performance loss | `tests/test_expected_goals.py` |
| `recent_form_index` | Recent competitive performance with regression to mean. | 0-1 | Results, xG, opponent strength, venue | Derived from `recent_form` | EWM with shrinkage; fallback normalizes rating | `src/simulation/expected_goals.py` | xG modifier, sensitivity | `tests/test_expected_goals.py` |
| `venue_context` | Environmental and travel context. | Multiplicative stress index | Venue altitude, climate, travel | Existing venue defaults | `temperature + humidity + altitude + travel` stress | `src/simulation/expected_goals.py`, `fatigue.py`, `injuries.py` | fatigue/injury summaries | `tests/test_fatigue_model.py` |
| `fatigue_current` | Accumulated physical load. | 0 fresh to 1 critical | Minutes, rest, travel, climate, pressing | 0 at tournament start | Clamped additive load minus recovery | `src/simulation/fatigue.py` | average fatigue, xG modifier | `tests/test_fatigue_model.py` |
| `injury_probability` | Probability of injury event. | 0-1 | Player workload, age, position, climate | Team-level aggregate fallback | Base probability times age, fatigue, load, climate, intensity | `src/simulation/injuries.py` | injuries by team/position, severe probability | `tests/test_injury_model.py` |
| `yellow_card_probability` | Probability of yellow card. | 0-1 per player/event | Player risk, team discipline, referee | Team aggregate fallback | Base card rate times player/team/ref/game-state modifiers | `src/simulation/cards.py` | cards, fair play tie-breaker | `tests/test_cards_fatigue.py` |
| `shootout_conversion_probability` | Penalty shootout conversion chance. | 0.50-0.95 | Taker skill, goalkeeper saves, fatigue, pressure | Team penalty rating and goalkeeper rating | Base + skill - goalkeeper - pressure - fatigue | `src/simulation/penalties.py` | shootout rate, winner | `tests/test_penalties.py` |
| `tactical_matchup_modifier` | Style advantage in a specific matchup. | 0.90-1.10 | Tactical scouting | Matrix fallback | `1 + matchup_advantage` clamped | `src/simulation/tactical_matchups.py` | xG adjustment | `tests/test_tactical_matchups.py` |
| `data_quality_score` | Reliability of inputs. | 0-1 | Source metadata | Inferred by missing/estimated/synthetic fields | Weighted completeness/reliability | `src/analytics/data_quality.py` | `data_quality_report.csv`, full report warning | `tests/test_data_quality.py` |
| `probability_ci` | Monte Carlo uncertainty. | Probability interval | Simulation counts | Wilson/normal approximation | `p ± 1.96 * sqrt(p(1-p)/n)` | `src/analytics/uncertainty.py` | `standard_error`, `ci_low`, `ci_high` | `tests/test_uncertainty.py` |
